Introduction to the Ocean

The ocean covers about 71% of the Earth's surface. It is vital to the survival of life on our planet, providing food, oxygen, and regulating climate.

Marine Life

Marine life includes all forms of life that live in the ocean. These range from tiny plankton to massive whales.

Ocean Depths

The ocean is divided into different zones, each hosting unique species. The deepest point on Earth is the Mariana Trench.

Conservation of the Ocean

The ocean is facing multiple threats, including pollution and climate change. Conservation efforts are essential for protecting marine life.

Fun Facts about the Ocean

Did you know that the Great Barrier Reef is the largest living structure on Earth, visible from space?

Why Protecting the Ocean is Vital

The health of the ocean directly impacts the entire planet. Oceans regulate climate, provide oxygen, and sustain the biodiversity that supports life on Earth.

Ocean Trivia

Over 80% of the ocean is unexplored and unmapped. The ocean remains one of the last great frontiers on Earth!
